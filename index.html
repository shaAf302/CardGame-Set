Instructions

Click to select 3 cards that are either all the same or all different for each of their properties. The 4 properties are:

  1. Color
  2. Number (of symbols)
  3. Shape
  4. Shading

If your selection is not a valid set, an explanation will appear to the right of the game area. Click selected cards to deselect them. Sometimes no SETs will be present among the cards on board, in these cases click the deck to reshuffle and deal 12 new cards. Note that this will only happen if no SETs are present (sometimes they are very hard to see!)
